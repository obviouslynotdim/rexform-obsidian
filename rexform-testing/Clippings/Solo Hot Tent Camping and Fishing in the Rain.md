In the heart of untouched nature, I tried to catch my dinner while fishing – which proved more difficult than expected, as the fish seemed just as affected by the cold weather as I was.  
Still, I was able to fully enjoy the peaceful atmosphere of the lake. A day full of rain, soothing sounds of nature, and the unique feeling of being completely alone in the wilderness.
